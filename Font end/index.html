    කාර්යක්ෂම සහ විනිවිද පෙනෙන ඉඩම් පරිපාලනය සඳහා ඔබේ දොරටුව වන ඉඩම් අංශය සොයා ගන්න. ඉඩම් වාර්තා යාවත්කාලීන කිරීම, දේපල තක්සේරු කිරීම සහ තීරණාත්මක ලේඛන කළමනාකරණය කිරීම සඳහා අපගේ පද්ධතිය බාධාවකින් තොර අත්දැකීමක් සපයයි. ඉඩම් කළමනාකරණ කාර්ය ප්‍රවාහය ප්‍රශස්ත කිරීමට, ප්‍රවේශ්‍යතාවය පෝෂණය කිරීමට සහ පරිපාලකයන්ට සහ මහජනතාවට විශ්වාසදායක වේදිකාවක් සහතික කිරීමට අපි කැපවී සිටිමු.

    about Kotapola Divisional secretariat

    in 1992 it widen as the Divisional Secretariat. From the outset it has been started with few officers and widen gradually. At present there are large number of officers exceeding hundred are being worked.

    • A Divisional Secretariat is a vital local administrative entity responsible for coordinating government activities at the divisional level. It acts as an intermediary between the central government and local communities, ensuring the effective delivery of public services and implementation of policies. The Divisional Secretariat plays a pivotal role in addressing local needs, managing resources, and fostering community development within its designated jurisdiction.
    • Kotapola Divisional secretariat called “Punchi Nuwaraeliya”which centered Deniyaya area,spread out about 175.50.square kilometres in extent in geographical distribution, situated in a beautiful environment in which connected to Sinharaja world heritage rainy forest and it is corner of Matara District in the Southern Province.

    Vision

    To be the premier Divisional Secretariat of the Island in 2020 through the excellent Public service

    Mission

    Providing solutions to public needs efficiently, fairly and harmoniously in accordance with government policies for the national development of the local secretariat and its community. Effective coordination of resources and institutions through well-planned strategies.
